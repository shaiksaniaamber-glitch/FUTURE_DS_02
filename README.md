# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes customer retention and churn patterns using customer subscription data. The objective is to identify the key factors influencing customer churn, understand customer behavior, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer retention. The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, churn analysis, retention metrics, and an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Objectives
- Clean and preprocess customer data.
- Analyze customer churn and retention trends.
- Identify customer segments with high churn.
- Understand customer lifetime patterns.
- Provide business recommendations to improve customer retention.
- Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for visualization.

## Key Insights
- Customers with month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate.
- Long-term customers are more likely to stay with the company.
- Monthly charges significantly impact churn.
- Customer retention improves with longer subscription periods.
- Business strategies should focus on customer engagement and loyalty.

## Business Recommendations
- Offer discounts for long-term subscriptions.
- Improve customer onboarding experience.
- Enhance customer support services.
- Introduce loyalty and reward programs.
- Monitor high-risk customers proactively.
